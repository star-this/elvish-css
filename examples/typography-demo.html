Philosophy

Typography is about readability, hierarchy, and voice. It's separate from visual effects (<i-thir>) and layout (<i-hath>, etc.). <i-tew> handles fonts, sizes, spacing, and text styling. 

<!-- Basic usage -->
<i-tew family="serif" size="lg" weight="bold">Elegant Heading</i-tew>

<!-- Spacing control -->
<i-tew leading="loose" tracking="wide">Spacious text</i-tew>

<!-- Transform and alignment -->
<i-tew transform="uppercase" align="center" block>CENTERED TITLE</i-tew>

<!-- Presets for common patterns -->
